Dwell

Dwell consolidates all the elements of living with a large group of people in one place. Primarily geared towards college students living off campus, this app helps you with everything from paying rent, scheduling, and even chore charts. Dwell relieves the frustrations and confusion that come with having housemates like no other app has done before.
