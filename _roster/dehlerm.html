Coalesce

Coalesce is a two-day conference for designers who want to use their talents to create change in the world. The conference will allow them to meet design allies and be inspired to create meaningful work and lasting change. I dreamed up, named, and created a visual identity for this conference.

Bussable

Navigate the city by bus based on advice from real people, not computers. Bussable is a mobile app that allows users to find the most reliable and fastest bus routes, see route reviews, and customize their ride based on user-generated content. Bus confident!

Acne sucks.

Although acne affects most of us, we do not know how deep it really goes. This infographic poster goes beneath the surface, taking viewers on a journey of understanding the mental and emotional effects of acne. I performed firsthand and secondhand research in order to develop this project.
