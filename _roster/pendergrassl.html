Colophon

You can interact with the Colophon prototype. Colophon is currently a mobile-only front end prototype.

Information about calligraphy is spread all over the dang place. I believe that this information should be easily accessible and shared; same for the wonderful pieces that people create. Colophon allows users to access and share their pieces, knowledge, and insights about anything calligraphy or lettering related.

SPU Repsonsive Retrofit

In August 2015, SPU re-launched the main part of it's website in a responsive format. SPU worked with digital firm Domain 7 to complete the retrofit. I helped out in small ways across the site, but a project I had all to myself was the coding of new responsive views for the SPU Master Calendar.

Graphogrammetry

Graphogrammetry is a fictional conference focused on bridging the gap between designer and developer. An identity, conference booklet, and poster for Graphogrammetry were developed within the Visual Communications 3 course at Seattle Pacific University, which focused on the theme of Design Research.
