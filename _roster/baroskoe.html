Pioneer: Design Research Conference

Pioneer’s mission is to educate and encourage designers to see design from a new perspective – one originating from the human brain. At this conference we want to examine how humans react to design from a very psychological perspective, and how we as designers can create with that mind.

Whim the App

Whim is an app for artists and creative people alike that can help one to find and cultivate inspiration. Whim complies and generates resources in one place so the user can more efficiently execute a project.

The Lingua Journal: Fall 2015-Winter 2016

The Lingua Journal is Seattle Pacific University's art and literary journal. It features original artwork, poetry, and prose by members of the SPU community. The goal of this journal is to bring art to the people; to light the fire of the artist community of SPU. Be a place of inclusion; a safe place for new artists to come and be encouraged.
