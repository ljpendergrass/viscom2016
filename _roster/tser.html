Breathe: An App for College Students

College is a time of change and big ideas, but how do we keep track of the little, but important things that keep us sane and healthy? Breathe helps college students track their daily moods and emotions, food intake, and provides a natural relaxation technique of deep breathing to help students with stress.

Scraps: A New Way to Cook

Generating new recipes based off of pre-existing ingredients in the fridge, Scraps uses these leftover ingredients to encourage creative cooking while minimizing food waste.

What the Font Promo Poster

What The Font is a two day typography conference held in New York City. Designed for typographers as well as any typography lover, WTF is a college audience focused event that studies typography's role in our past, present, and future. Attendees will learn how to build and create type, while exploring legibility and readability.
